Our Work

At Seven Star Holdings, we are in the business of empowerment. We empower hotel, resort, timeshare, and residence owners across the globe by offering top-notch management and marketing services for unsold inventory and entire properties on popular platforms like Airbnb and VRBO. Our hands-on approach means we manage every facet, from the intricate property listing process to ensuring guests depart with unforgettable experiences. Our commitment to quality is evident, having earned prestigious industry accolades such as the Airbnb Superhost and VRBO Premier Host titles. But our expertise does not end with property management. We extend our white-glove services to the travel realm, providing bespoke experiences for individuals, professionals, and families. From tailored property searches to the curation of personalized travel guidebooks, we are the solitary point of contact, ensuring a seamless and enriched travel experience for our clients. Our unique positioning allows us to meld the worlds of travel and property management, bestowing our clientele with unmatched discounts, perks, and benefits. The culmination of our efforts is the Seven Star Club, our exclusive loyalty program designed to celebrate and reward our clients 'enduring loyalty.
